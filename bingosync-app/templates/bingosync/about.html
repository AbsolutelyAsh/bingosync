Bingosync
What is Bingosync?

Bingosync is a web application that lets people collaboratively work on "bingo boards" for speedrunning games.

When you create a bingosync room from the main page of bingosync.com, you will get a unique URL that you can share with other people to let them join your room. When multiple people are in the same room the squares on the bingoboard is synchronized between them in real time, letting you plan and keep track of bingo progress together.

Common applications of bingosync include team bingo "blackout" races like last year's team bingo league, this year's international bingo league, spontaneous team bingo blackouts, and competetive bingo "lockouts".

About Bingo and Bingosync

As far as I'm aware, the practice of speedrunning bingo races originated on speedrunslive.com. You can view the speedrunslive bingo generators here. Bingosync's generators are copied from speedrunslive so you can get the same board on each site by using the same seed.

About a year ago, the popular solution for "bingo-syncing" was bingosync.twitchtools.tv which appears to now be defunct. I wrote bingosync.com in order to replace bingosync.twitchtools.tv when I learned that it had shut down. Maybe someday I'll buy the twitchtools.tv domain and have it redirect to bingosync.com :)

The source code for Bingosync is hosted on github, feel free to check it out or message me about it!
